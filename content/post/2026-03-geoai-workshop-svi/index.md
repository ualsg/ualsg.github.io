We are happy to announce a workshop that will be part of the [1st International Conference on Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI 2026)](https://geoaiconference.org/), which will take place in Ghent, Belgium on 3-6 June 2026.
We welcome abstracts on the topic.

Organisers: {{% mention "filip" %}}, {{% mention "xiaobing-wei" %}}, {{% mention "pengyuan" %}}, Weiming Huang, Fangli Guan, and Stephen Law.

Deadline: 30 March 2026.

More information: see [here](https://geoaiconference.org/?page_id=200).

GeoAI 2026 is organised by [The GeoAI Research Center at Ghent University](https://geoaiugent.wordpress.com/) (chairs: Haosheng Huang and Nico Van de Weghe).
